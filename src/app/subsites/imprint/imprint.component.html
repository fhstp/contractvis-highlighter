Object of the company

Preparing and implementing university of applied sciences degree programmes and operating a university of applied sciences in St. Pölten with several degree programmes, all in accordance with the objectives and basic principles of university of applied sciences degrees as specified in section 3 Fachhochschul-Studiengesetz (University of Applied Sciences Studies Act). Rendering services to third parties in fields where the company conducts research and teaching, running degree programmes and continuing education courses, and operating student accommodation and a student canteen. Within the scope of these activities, the company is authorised to conduct all such transactions and take all such measures as are deemed necessary to achieve the company’s objectives. The company exclusively and directly serves public-benefit purposes in the meaning of the Bundesabgabenordnung (Austrian Fiscal Code); its activities are not aimed at generating profit. The company’s resources may only be used for the purpose of achieving the university’s public-benefit objectives.

Shareholders/investments

The shareholders of St. Pölten UAS are Hochschulen St. Pölten Holding GmbH (FN 467878 g; holding 74% of the shares), whose sole shareholder is the Municipality of St. Pölten, and the Province of Lower Austria (holding 26% of the shares).

Basic objectives

To promote and advertise St. Pölten UAS and report on matters relating to the teaching and research undertaken as part of the degree programmes offered by the university.

Use of the website

Already with every visit and every use of our homepage we store the following data:

  • IP address
  • Date and time of the request
  • controlled subpage on our website
  • Type and version of the browser
  • Used terminal
  • operating system
  • Referrer URL

The purpose of this data processing is the maintenance of our website, in particular a faster error localization and correction, as well as the monitoring of the utilization and, if necessary, make adjustments or improvements. If a personal reference is given at all, the data security to be granted and the maintenance of the online presentation represent our legitimate interests within the meaning of Article 6 (1) (f) DS-GVO and thus the legal basis. The data (with the exception of the IP address) will only become stored for a limited duration and a personal reference is not produced from this data, because the associated IP address is deleted or at least anonymized. The IP address of your computer will also be stored only for the time of your use of the website and then deleted or anonymized by shortening. Only in the case of hacking attacks or the like, a longer storage may be necessary to ensure the functionality and security of our website. A transmission of your data to third parties does not take place.

Use of cookies on this website

"Cookies" are small files that allow us to store on your PC specific information related to you, the user, while you visit one of our websites. Cookies help us to determine the frequency of use and the number of users of our websites, as well as to make our offers as comfortable and efficient as possible for you. The content of the cookies we use is limited to an identification number that no longer allows any personal reference to the user.

A use of our offers is also possible without cookies. You can disable the storage of cookies in your browser, restrict them to certain websites or set your browser to notify them when a cookie is sent. You can also delete cookies from your PC's hard drive at any time. Please note, however, that in this case you will have to reckon with a limited display of the page and limited user guidance.
